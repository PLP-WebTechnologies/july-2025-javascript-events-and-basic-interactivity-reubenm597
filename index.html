Introduction

Remote work, also known as telework or telecommuting, refers to a flexible work arrangement where employees perform their job duties from an alternative worksite (such as home or a co-working space) rather than regularly commuting to a central office . This mode of work has evolved significantly, especially after the COVID-19 pandemic, and continues to shape modern work culture.
